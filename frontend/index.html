ℹ️ How are agents & groups measured? click to expand
Agent QA Score (0–100)

Mean QA score across all evaluated tickets. Each ticket is scored by Claude Haiku on 8 dimensions (1–5 each). Total = average × 20. Minimum 3 tickets required for a reliable average. Colour coding: 🔴 below 55 · 🟡 55–74 · 🟢 75+.

Churn Risk Rate

% of this agent's tickets carrying a churn flag. Three-tier detection: Salesforce CRM tag (confirmed termination) → Claude AI inference (explicit cancellation intent) → specific keyword + ticket score <70. Team baseline: 18.6%.

Bad CX Rate

% of tickets with any bad CX signal: AI-detected repeat contact, conversation length >2 turns, or churn flag. This is a proxy for customer friction — not a direct CSAT measurement.

Group measurements

Groups (Customer Care, Classic Integrations, Capitech etc.) aggregate the same metrics across all agents in that Freshdesk group. Group assignment comes from Freshdesk's group_id field, resolved against the groups API at evaluation time. Tickets with no group appear as "Unknown".

Risk profile labels
  • ⚠️ QA/Churn mismatch — QA ≥70 but churn ≥18.6%. High scores not preventing churn.
  • 🔴 High risk — QA <59 AND churn ≥18.6%.
  • 🟠 Watch — churn above baseline, QA not critically low.
  • ✅ Low risk — QA ≥70 and churn below baseline.
  • 🟡 Average — both metrics within normal range.
What is not measured here
  • Response/resolution time (in Freshdesk but not yet surfaced)
  • Ticket volume vs team capacity
  • CSAT (not currently returned by Freshdesk API)
  • Score trends over time (requires multiple completed runs)

ℹ️ How is this measured? click to expand
Data source

Aggregates all evaluated tickets for the current loaded dataset. Tags come from Freshdesk ticket fields. Each ticket may carry multiple tags and is counted once per tag. Minimum 5 tickets per tag to be included.

Volume

Distinct tickets with this tag that have a QA evaluation. Tags below 5 tickets are excluded as statistically unreliable.

Churn Risk %

Churn-flagged tickets / total tickets in this category x 100. Three-tier churn detection: Salesforce CRM tag (confirmed termination) > Claude AI inference (explicit cancellation intent) > specific keyword + QA score <70. Team baseline: 18.6%.

Risk Level thresholds
  • High — churn rate ≥30%
  • Med — churn rate 15–29.9%
  • Low — churn rate <15%

Absolute thresholds, not relative to the team baseline. Always read volume and rate together — a low-volume category with a 90% rate may have only 5 tickets.

ARR at Risk

Sum of ARR for companies with churn-flagged tickets in this category, counted once per unique company. ARR is fetched from the Freshdesk company record at evaluation time. Shows [ARR unknown] when no ARR data was available for churn tickets in this category.

Top 5

The 5 rows with the highest churn rate are highlighted with a left-border accent. Note that the highest churn rate is not always the highest business risk — also consider volume and ARR.
